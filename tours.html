Tours in Tulum

Historical Riches

In the 13th century, Tulum was a vital maritime port for Mayan merchants. Currently, this destination reveals a fascinating history that deserves to be explored by all its visitors.

Beach and Adventure

Tulum, an essential part of the Riviera Maya, protects some of the best-preserved archaeological sites. Less than two hours from Cancun International Airport, this place offers direct connections from the airport to Tulum or Playa del Carmen, ideal for excursions to nearby cenotes, lagoons, and archaeological sites.

City Exploration

No matter where you stay in Tulum, you'll always have attractions within walking distance. The city is conducive to walking, with bicycles being the most common mode of transportation. From excursions to the lush Mayan jungle to discoveries in Mayan communities, the city offers unique experiences, while the Sian Ka'an Biosphere Reserve offers a natural sanctuary accessible from the picturesque village of Muyil.

Tours in Playa del Carmen

Caribbean Treasures

Playa del Carmen, epicenter of the Riviera Maya, is a cosmopolitan destination that has managed to preserve the Caribbean authenticity that has catapulted it to fame. With impressive beaches, a varied culinary offering, lively nightlife, captivating natural and theme parks, as well as enigmatic cenotes in its vicinity, the city offers a wide range of experiences.

Unique Adventures

Immerse yourself in unique experiences by interacting with local fauna, such as swimming with turtles in Akumal (May to September), diving with bull sharks (November to March), or encountering crocodiles in a cenote at night, an experience reserved for the most intrepid. Whether with family, friends, as a couple, or alone, dare to explore all the wonders that Playa del Carmen and its surroundings have in store for you.

Natural Adventure

For nature and adventure lovers, Playa del Carmen and its surroundings offer a wide range of activities in impressive natural settings. From bird watching and ATV rides to zip line circuits and skydiving, "Playa" stands out as the only destination in the Mexican Caribbean where you can enjoy the latter activity.
